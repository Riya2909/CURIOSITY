OUR MOTIVE

Sex education is a very important type of education, especially for teenagers. In India, there is a crucial need for sexual awareness among people. Lack of proper education can be very harmful for the mental as well as physical health of people. It is often observed that wrong presumptions about sex and other related topics can have adverse effects on teenagers. The taboo of sex makes it even worse.

Our main motive through this website is to provide sex education to people in a proper and decent manner. We want to spread sexual awareness among teenagers to help them understand the changes in their body during puberty. Though schools do provide this education but we often find teachers either missing out these topics. Our website makes sure that no content is in any way offensive or vulgar.
